Taco Recipe

Today we're going to be making a classic Bryan Taco! This recipe is really simple! The ingredients you're gonna need are:

  • Ground Beef
  • Soft Tortillas
  • Cheese
  • Iceberg Lettuce
  • Sour Cream
  • Hot Sauce
  • Optional Seasonings: Cumin, Paprika, Garlic Powder, Onion Powder

      1. Cook the ground beef, seasoning it to taste.
      2. Heat the tortilla in the microwave, optionally with cheese.
      3. Add the ground beef on to the tortilla, with cheese, sour cream, lettuce, and hot sauce.
      4. Enjoy!
